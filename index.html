Welcome to the HAU Orchestra

The HAU Orchestra is a renowned musical ensemble, open to musicians of all ages and levels. If you’re passionate about music and want to be part of a dedicated team, we’re looking for you! Under the guidance of conductor Sir Edwin, our orchestra performs a wide range of classical and modern compositions.
